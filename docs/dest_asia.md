## General Asia Travel Tips

- **Vaccinations**: Hepatitis A recommended for most Asian destinations. Typhoid, Japanese encephalitis, and rabies recommended for rural areas. Consult your doctor 8 weeks before travel.
- **Currency**: Carry some local cash as many markets and local restaurants are cash-only. ATMs are widely available in cities.
- **Dress code**: Cover shoulders and knees when visiting temples throughout Asia.
- **Water**: Drink only bottled or filtered water in Southeast Asia and parts of South Asia.
- **Bargaining**: Normal practice in markets in Thailand, Vietnam, Bali, and India. Not appropriate in malls or restaurants.
- **Scams**: Use only metered or app-based taxis (Grab is recommended across Southeast Asia). Book tours through your hotel or SunPath to avoid scams.
- **Time zones**: Asia spans UTC+5:30 (India) to UTC+9 (Japan). Plan for significant jet lag if coming from the Americas.

# Cancellation and Refund Policy — SunPath Travel Agency

## Overview

SunPath Travel Agency understands that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our clients and our supplier partners who impose their own cancellation deadlines.

All cancellation requests must be submitted in writing via email to cancellations@sunpath-travel.com or through your online account at www.sunpath-travel.com/my-bookings.

---

## Standard Cancellation Policy

### Package Tours and Hotel Bookings

| Days Before Departure | Cancellation Fee |
|----------------------|-----------------|
| 60+ days | 10% of total package price (admin fee) |
| 45–59 days | 25% of total package price |
| 30–44 days | 50% of total package price |
| 15–29 days | 75% of total package price |
| 0–14 days | 100% of total package price (no refund) |

### Flight-Only Bookings

Flight cancellation fees are determined by the airline and fare type:
- **Flexible fares**: Full refund minus $50 processing fee
- **Standard fares**: 50% refund if cancelled 7+ days before departure; no refund within 7 days
- **Non-refundable fares**: No cash refund; credit voucher valid for 12 months may be issued

---

## Refund Process

1. Submit your cancellation request in writing
2. Our team will confirm receipt within 24 business hours
3. Refund eligibility is calculated based on the date we receive your written request
4. Approved refunds are processed within 10–14 business days
5. Refunds are returned to the original payment method

---

## Cancellation Due to Illness or Emergency

If you must cancel due to a serious medical emergency or the death of an immediate family member:
- Provide official documentation (medical certificate or death certificate)
- We will waive up to 50% of applicable cancellation fees
- If you have travel insurance, your insurer may cover the remaining costs
- Requests must be submitted within 48 hours of the emergency

---

## Trip Cancellation by SunPath

In the rare event that SunPath cancels a tour (e.g., due to insufficient bookings, natural disaster, or government travel advisory):
- You will be notified at least 30 days before departure when possible
- Full refund of all payments made, processed within 7 business days
- OR the option to transfer to an alternative tour of equal or lesser value
- SunPath is not liable for non-refundable expenses booked independently (flights, visas, etc.)

---

## Force Majeure

SunPath is not liable for cancellations caused by events beyond our control including:
- Natural disasters (earthquakes, floods, hurricanes)
- War, terrorism, or civil unrest
- Pandemic or government-mandated travel restrictions
- Strikes or industrial action affecting transport providers

In force majeure situations, we will make every effort to reschedule or issue travel credits. Cash refunds in force majeure cases are subject to what we can recover from suppliers.

---

## How to Cancel

**Online**: Log in at www.sunpath-travel.com/my-bookings → Select booking → Click "Request Cancellation"

**By Email**: Send to cancellations@sunpath-travel.com with:
- Full name
- Booking reference number
- Departure date
- Reason for cancellation

**By Phone**: Call +1-800-555-0199 (Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm EST) — note that verbal requests must be followed by written confirmation to be processed.

---

## Name Changes and Transfers

Instead of cancelling, you may transfer your booking to another person:
- Name change fee: $75 per traveler
- Must be requested at least 21 days before departure
- Both original and new traveler must provide passport details
- Not available for non-refundable flight bookings

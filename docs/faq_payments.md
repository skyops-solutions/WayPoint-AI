# Payments, Refunds, and Pricing FAQ — SunPath Travel Agency

## Payments

**Q: When do I need to pay for my trip?**
A: A 30% deposit is required at booking to confirm your reservation. The remaining 70% is due 45 days before departure. If you book within 45 days of departure, full payment is required immediately.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: We accept:
- Visa, MasterCard, American Express (no surcharge)
- PayPal (+1.5% processing fee)
- Bank wire transfer (required for bookings over $10,000)
- Installment plan via our financing partner (0% interest for 6 months on packages $2,000+)

**Q: Is my payment secure?**
A: Yes. All payments are processed through PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant systems. We never store your full card number. Online payments use 3D Secure authentication.

**Q: Can I pay in installments?**
A: Yes, for packages priced at $2,000 or more. We offer:
- 3-month plan: 0% interest
- 6-month plan: 0% interest
- 12-month plan: 4.9% APR
Contact our billing department at billing@sunpath-travel.com to set up a plan.

**Q: Will I be charged in my local currency?**
A: All SunPath prices are quoted in USD. If your card is in a different currency, your bank applies its own exchange rate. To avoid surprise fees, use a card with no foreign transaction fees (e.g., Wise, Revolut, or Schwab).

## Refunds

**Q: How long do refunds take?**
A: Approved refunds are processed within 10–14 business days. The time to appear in your account depends on your bank (usually 3–5 additional business days after processing).

**Q: Can I get a refund if I need to cancel?**
A: Yes, depending on how far in advance you cancel. See our full Cancellation Policy. In summary:
- 60+ days before departure: 90% refund (10% admin fee kept)
- 45–59 days: 75% refund
- 30–44 days: 50% refund
- 15–29 days: 25% refund
- 0–14 days: No refund

**Q: What if my flight is cancelled by the airline?**
A: If the airline cancels the flight, you are entitled to a full refund of the flight portion from the airline under EU261 or US DOT regulations. SunPath will assist you in submitting the claim. Hotel and tour portions are subject to standard cancellation terms.

**Q: I paid by bank transfer. How do I get a refund?**
A: Refunds for bank transfer payments are returned by bank transfer. Provide your bank account details when submitting your cancellation. International wire transfers may take up to 21 business days and may incur a $15–$25 fee charged by your bank.

**Q: My trip was worse than described. Can I get a partial refund?**
A: If a significant component of your trip was not as described (e.g., hotel was of lower category, a tour was cancelled without alternative), you may be entitled to partial compensation. Submit a formal complaint within 28 days of returning: complaints@sunpath-travel.com. Include: booking reference, description, and supporting evidence (photos, receipts).

## Pricing

**Q: Are prices per person or for the whole group?**
A: All prices listed are per person based on double occupancy (two people sharing a room). Single supplement charges apply if you are traveling alone and want a private room — typically 25–40% extra.

**Q: What's included in the price?**
A: Standard package prices include:
- Round-trip economy flights
- Accommodation as specified
- Airport transfers (arrival and departure)
- Daily breakfast
- Guided tours as listed in the itinerary
- Basic travel insurance
- Baggage fees for one checked bag

Not included: Visa fees, optional excursions, tips, personal spending, meals beyond breakfast.

**Q: Why did the price change since I last checked?**
A: Package prices fluctuate based on flight availability and hotel inventory — similar to booking directly with airlines. Prices are only guaranteed once you pay your deposit. We recommend booking early for the best rates.

**Q: Do you offer a price match guarantee?**
A: Yes. If you find an identical package (same flights, same hotel category, same dates) for less elsewhere within 48 hours of your SunPath booking, we will match the price. Submit the comparison quote to pricematch@sunpath-travel.com.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: No hidden fees. The price you see includes all airline-imposed fees, hotel taxes, and our service fee. The only additional charges are:
- Visa fees (if applicable to your destination)
- Optional activities and upgrades you choose to add
- Premium insurance upgrade (if selected)
- Single supplement (if traveling solo and requesting a private room)

## Group Bookings

**Q: What discounts apply to group bookings?**
A:
- 10–19 travelers: 10% discount on package price
- 20+ travelers: 15% discount on package price
- Corporate groups (company retreats, incentive travel): contact corporate@sunpath-travel.com for custom pricing

**Q: Is a deposit required for group bookings?**
A: For groups of 10–19, a 20% deposit secures the group rate. For 20+, we require a 15% deposit. Full payment is due 60 days before departure for groups (vs 45 days for individual bookings).

**Q: Can group members pay separately?**
A: Yes. We can send individual payment links to each traveler. One group leader must be designated as the primary contact and is responsible for ensuring all payments are made on time.

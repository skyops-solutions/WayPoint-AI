# Frequently Asked Questions — SunPath Travel Agency

## General Questions

**Q: What is SunPath Travel Agency?**
A: SunPath Travel Agency is a full-service travel company offering vacation packages, flight bookings, hotel reservations, guided tours, and travel insurance. We specialize in European, Asian, and Mediterranean destinations.

**Q: How do I contact customer support?**
A: You can reach us via:
- Phone: +1-800-555-0199 (Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm EST)
- Email: support@sunpath-travel.com
- Live chat on our website at www.sunpath-travel.com
- In person at any of our 12 offices across the country

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and bank wire transfers. For packages over $5,000 we also offer installment payment plans with 0% interest for the first 6 months.

**Q: Can I book a trip for someone else?**
A: Yes. You can book on behalf of another person. You will need to provide their full legal name (as it appears on their passport), date of birth, and passport number at the time of booking.

**Q: Do you offer group discounts?**
A: Yes. Groups of 10 or more travelers receive a 10% discount on package tours. Groups of 20 or more receive a 15% discount. Contact our group travel desk at groups@sunpath-travel.com for a custom quote.

**Q: Is travel insurance included in packages?**
A: Basic travel insurance is included in all packages priced above $1,500. This covers emergency medical expenses up to $50,000 and trip cancellation due to illness. Premium insurance with broader coverage is available as an add-on for $79–$149 per traveler.

**Q: What documents do I need to travel?**
A: Requirements vary by destination. In general:
- Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity beyond travel dates)
- Visa (if required — our agents will advise)
- Travel insurance certificate
- Vaccination records (if required by destination)

**Q: How far in advance should I book?**
A: We recommend booking at least 3 months in advance for peak season travel (June–August, December–January). For off-season trips, 4–6 weeks in advance is usually sufficient. Last-minute deals are available but subject to availability.

**Q: Can I customize a package?**
A: Absolutely. All our packages can be customized — you can adjust hotel category, add excursions, change departure dates, or extend your stay. Contact your travel consultant to discuss options.

**Q: What happens if my flight is delayed or cancelled by the airline?**
A: If your flight is delayed or cancelled by the airline, our 24/7 emergency line (+1-800-555-0911) will assist you with rebooking. If the delay causes you to miss connecting transfers or hotel check-in, we will cover the cost of alternative arrangements up to $300 per traveler.

## Booking Process

**Q: How does the booking process work?**
A:
1. Browse our packages online or consult with an agent
2. Select your destination, dates, and traveler count
3. Receive a detailed quote within 24 hours
4. Pay a 30% deposit to confirm the booking
5. Pay the remaining balance 45 days before departure
6. Receive your travel documents 7–10 days before departure

**Q: What is included in a package price?**
A: Standard packages include:
- Round-trip flights (economy class)
- Hotel accommodation (as specified)
- Airport transfers
- Daily breakfast
- One guided city tour

Not included: personal spending, optional excursions, visa fees, tips.

**Q: Can I see the itinerary before booking?**
A: Yes. We provide a full day-by-day itinerary before you confirm and pay the deposit. You can request changes to the itinerary at no charge before payment.

**Q: How do I get my tickets and travel documents?**
A: All documents are sent electronically to your registered email address 7–10 days before departure. You will receive: e-tickets, hotel vouchers, transfer confirmations, and your travel insurance certificate. Physical copies are available on request.

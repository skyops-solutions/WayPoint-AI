# Booking Terms and Conditions — SunPath Travel Agency

## 1. Booking Confirmation

A booking is confirmed only when:
- SunPath has received your completed booking form with accurate traveler details
- A deposit of 30% of the total price has been paid and cleared
- You have received a written booking confirmation with a unique reference number

Verbal or informal agreements (phone, chat) do not constitute a confirmed booking.

---

## 2. Deposit and Final Payment

- **Deposit**: 30% of total package price is due at the time of booking
- **Final Payment**: The remaining 70% is due no later than **45 days before departure**
- For bookings made within 45 days of departure, full payment is required immediately
- Late payment may result in automatic cancellation of the booking without refund of deposit

Payment reminders are sent 60, 45, and 30 days before the due date.

---

## 3. Price Guarantee

Once your booking is confirmed and deposit paid:
- The package price is locked in and will not increase
- Exceptions: government-imposed taxes, fuel surcharges, or currency fluctuations exceeding 5% on international packages
- If a price increase of more than 10% occurs, you have the right to cancel with a full refund

---

## 4. Traveler Information Requirements

You must provide accurate information for each traveler:
- Full legal name (exactly as on passport)
- Date of birth
- Passport number and expiry date
- Nationality
- Emergency contact details
- Dietary requirements or accessibility needs

Errors in traveler details may result in airline or hotel penalties. Amendment fees of $50–$200 per change may apply depending on the supplier.

---

## 5. Passports and Visas

- It is the traveler's responsibility to ensure their passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond the return travel date
- SunPath will advise on visa requirements but obtaining visas is the traveler's responsibility
- We offer a visa assistance service for $50 per person that includes form preparation and document checklist
- SunPath is not liable if a traveler is denied entry due to invalid documents or visa issues

---

## 6. Special Requests

Special requests (e.g., vegetarian meals, adjacent rooms, early check-in) will be communicated to suppliers but cannot be guaranteed. They do not form part of the contract.

Medical requirements and wheelchair/mobility assistance must be declared at booking. Airlines and hotels require advance notice for these services.

---

## 7. Itinerary Changes

SunPath reserves the right to modify itineraries due to operational reasons:
- Minor changes (hotel substitution of equal or higher category, order of tours) — no compensation
- Significant changes (destination change, 24+ hour schedule change) — you may cancel with full refund or accept an alternative

You will be notified of significant changes as soon as possible and no later than 14 days before departure.

---

## 8. Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend that all travelers purchase comprehensive travel insurance. SunPath's included basic insurance covers:
- Emergency medical and hospitalization: up to $50,000
- Emergency evacuation: up to $100,000
- Trip cancellation due to illness: up to 80% of trip cost

It does NOT cover: pre-existing medical conditions, adventure sports, loss of personal items, or trip delays due to personal reasons.

Upgrade to Premium Insurance ($79–$149/person) for:
- Pre-existing condition coverage (with prior declaration)
- Baggage loss up to $2,000
- Travel delay compensation ($100/day after 6-hour delay)
- Adventure sports coverage

---

## 9. Complaints and Disputes

If you have a complaint during your trip:
1. Contact your local SunPath representative or our 24/7 emergency line: +1-800-555-0911
2. Allow us the opportunity to resolve the issue on-site
3. If unresolved, submit a formal complaint within 28 days of return to complaints@sunpath-travel.com

Formal complaints are acknowledged within 5 business days and resolved within 28 business days. SunPath participates in ATAS (American Travel Arbitration Service) for unresolved disputes.

---

## 10. Liability

SunPath acts as an agent for airlines, hotels, and tour operators. Our liability is limited to:
- Direct losses caused by our proven negligence
- Maximum of the total package price paid

We are not liable for: delays, losses, or injuries caused by third-party suppliers, force majeure events, or traveler negligence.

---

## 11. Health and Fitness

You are responsible for ensuring you are fit to travel. SunPath recommends:
- Consulting your doctor before travel to remote or tropical destinations
- Checking vaccination requirements at least 8 weeks before departure
- Declaring any medical conditions that may affect your ability to participate in tour activities

Some tours have minimum fitness requirements. These are noted in the tour description.
